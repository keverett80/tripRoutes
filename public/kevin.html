About Us

Davis Development Enterprise is a holding company with multi-faceted transportation organizations designed to enhance two areas of needs in the community and region:

Freight Pickup and Delivery Services: Specialize in the Pickup and Delivery of all kinds of freight from consumer goods, retail merchandise, ammunition, pharmaceuticals, gardening equipment, etc., to assist manufacturers and distribution centers in providing for the flow of goods to the public.

Non-Emergency Medical Transportation Service: Specializing in safe, reliable, and compassionate transportation for individuals with medical needs. Our fleet includes 3 specialized wheelchair vans—one equipped for stretcher transport and another designed for multiple wheelchairs—ensuring we can meet a variety of client requirements. Our NEMT business thrives amidst high demand in a competitive landscape, distinguished by superior service and reliability. Leveraging advanced scheduling technology and a robust fleet, including specialized vans and medical equipment, we've carved a niche in the market. The sector's continuous growth signals vast opportunities for expansion and service diversification, even in a crowded marketplace.
